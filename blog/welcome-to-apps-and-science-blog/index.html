Welcome to the Apps and Science Blog!

Jan 7th, 2012

Welcome to the Apps and Science blog! This blog will document my progress towards learning how to create a science app for the iPhone and the iPad.

I am a chemist with some knowledge of programming but with a great interest in creating science apps. Thinking that “it can’t be that hard to create an app,” I got started reading about app development in December, 2011. I hope to develop my own science app by June, 2012.

The blog will not only document my progress but it will also provide answers to the following questions:

  • What are the types of science apps that exist on the Apple store?

  • What apps are there for Chemistry, Physics, and Biology?

  • How can I review existing science apps?

  • How hard is it to create an app?

  • What hardware do I need to create an app?

  • What computer language should I learn in order to develop an app?

  • What books teach app development?

  • Can I do advanced math on an Apple device?

  • How can I promote my app?

  • Can I expect to make money from making apps?

I will be updating this blog twice a week, on Monday and on Friday. So visit frequently or subscribe to this blog by using the RSS link to the right.
